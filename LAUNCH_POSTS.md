## LinkedIn Founder Post

We just open-sourced MST Open Tools: 15 browser tools for mature-node MPW planning, safer first RFQs, P&ID/SOLIDWORKS intake, UHP gas-stick review and sourcing cleanup.

The most important design rule is privacy at the first conversation:

- no GDS at intake
- no netlists, RTL, schematics or masks in a public form
- high-level node, process family, die area, sample quantity, package/test assumptions and timeline are enough to start screening

The toolset includes MPW RFQ Pack Builder, Tapeout Readiness Checker, Shuttle Schedule Finder, Local GDSII Inspector, GDS/OASIS Handoff Manifest, PDK Checklist, Reticle & Wafer Planner, Procurement Timeline Planner, P&ID Tag Parser, BOM Checker and BOM RFQ Normalizer.

MST is not a wafer foundry. We coordinate mature-node MPW RFQs: high-level intake, process-fit screening, PDK/NDA path clarification, packaging/test scoping and partner-confirmed quote routing.
