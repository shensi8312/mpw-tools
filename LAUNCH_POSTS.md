## LinkedIn Founder Post

We just open-sourced a small MPW planning toolkit for teams preparing mature-node tapeout RFQs.

It includes:

- MPW reticle & wafer planner
- Dies-per-wafer / cost estimator
- Local GDSII inspector

The GDSII inspector is the part I care about most: it runs entirely in the browser. It can read top cell, die size, layer list and cell hierarchy without uploading the file. The goal is to help teams prepare a non-confidential RFQ summary before sharing design IP.

MST is not a wafer foundry. We coordinate mature-node MPW RFQs: high-level intake, process-fit screening, PDK/NDA path clarification, packaging/test scoping, and partner-confirmed quote routing.

Tools: https://mst-sg.com/mpw/#tools
GitHub: https://github.com/shensi8312/mpw-tools

If you are planning an analog, RF, BCD/power, eNVM, sensor, IoT or mixed-signal prototype on mature-node MPW, this should help you frame the first conversation without sending GDS at intake.
